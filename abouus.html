About Us

based in Sai Gon


Hello! We are Cartoonè - A design team with four enthusiasm members who are interested in making every idea becomes "real" and "real-crazy".

The name "Cartoonè" means "This is cartoon" in Vietnamese. Cartoon is what connected us together. Not only because it was a part of was memorable childhood, but also the way each images worked made things become such a magical.


Based in Ho Chi Minh City (aka. Sai Gon), we are proudly inherited the positive mind and active spirit of the young Saigonese . We believe that nothing is impossible. Challenges and remarkable ideas are what we are looking for and waiting to deal with.
